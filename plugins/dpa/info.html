DPA N-Series Digital Wireless

Connects to a DPA N-Series wireless receiver (e.g. N-DR1) via OSC over TCP.

  • Default TCP port: 1993 (configurable on device)
  • Shows both channels with audio level, RF strength (A/B), battery status and active warnings
  • Audio level, RF strength and battery data are read from the device via OSC subscriptions and /settings/retrieve

Enable TCP control on the device under Advanced → LAN.
